In the quiet blue, I’m standing still,
Where sunlight drifts like falling silk.
Echoes of a world long gone,
Breathe softly through these broken halls.
呢片海，收起咗時間嘅愛與痛，
玻璃倒影裏面 誰 仲守住夢？
微光照向我，叫我再向前。
Carry me through this ocean of memory,
讓心跳喺深海重新聽得見。
When the tide rolls back,
We find what still remains—
一點光，一點暖，
守住未忘嘅從前。
沉落海底之中，故事變得好輕，
舊城沙石之內，仲聽到你嘅聲。
步向那束亮光，我心慢慢變清。
We walk through water
as if we’re walking through time—
and every step rewrites
what the world left behind.
光影穿過裂縫，把沉默變成願望，
Hold me close before it fades,
記住呢種心跳嘅重量。
In this glass cathedral sea,
你同我仍然未散落。
